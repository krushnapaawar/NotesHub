Trignometry

The Branch of Maths that delas with the study of of reationship between the sides and angle of triangle

1. Trigonometric Ratios (Basic Ratios)

1) sin θ = Opposite Side / Hypotenuse

2) cos θ = Adjacent Side / Hypotenuse

3) tan θ = Opposite Side / Adjacent Side

Reciprocal Ratios

4) cosec θ = Hypotenuse / Opposite Side = 1 / sin θ

5) sec θ = Hypotenuse / Adjacent Side = 1 / cos θ

6) cot θ = Adjacent Side / Opposite Side = 1 / tan θ

2. Trigonometric Identities

⇒ sin²θ + cos²θ = 1

⇒ 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ

⇒ 1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ
